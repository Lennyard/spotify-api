What is this program for?

This is a search engine to find similar artists to YOUR favorite artist. Here's what you do:

  • 1) Type your favorite artist, or any artist at all, and click on "GO!"
  • 2) If you typed in a specific artist, you will get that artist; but if you typed in an incomplete artist name, you will be greeted with many artists with that name to choose from.
  • 3) Click on the artist you want to find similar artists to. and...!
  • 4) You will see all the similar artists on the next page.

Hope you enjoy this program. So, ready to find similar artists to your musical taste?
